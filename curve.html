    "About BMC-IT"

    "Bmc-IT - stands for the IT(information tecnology) department of Bhairahawa Multiple Campus. shantinagar-16,bhairahawa,Rupandehi. Here we currently running Bsc-csit & BCA as IT related courses and thinking about bringing BIT course as soon as possible. Observing the fast growing world, Science and Technology work as Backbone of the world. So,we are also focused on technology to contribute our nation from our productive IT student's."

    Department's Message

    Bhairahawa Multiple Campus (BMC) is a pioneer campus of this region. It was established in 2025 V.S. to address the then need of the society.
